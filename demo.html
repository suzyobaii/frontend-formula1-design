Titillium Web Recommended

Formula 1 (F1) is the highest level of international motorsport for single-seater race cars.

It features teams like Ferrari, Mercedes, and Red Bull competing in a global championship.

Races, called Grands Prix, take place on tracks and city streets across various countries.

font-family: 'Titillium Web', sans-serif;

A great all-around choice for both headers and body text. Clean and technical.

Orbitron Futuristic

Formula 1 in 2025 is more competitive than ever, with teams pushing the boundaries of innovation.

font-family: 'Orbitron', sans-serif;

Excellent for counters, stats, and futuristic elements. Best used sparingly for maximum impact.

Rajdhani Clean & Modern

From Senna to Schumacher to Hamilton, F1 has seen some of the greatest athletes in motorsport history.

font-family: 'Rajdhani', sans-serif;

A clean, modern sans-serif with a technical feel. Good for both body text and headlines.

Exo 2 Geometric

F1 cars have evolved dramatically from their humble beginnings as simple open-wheel machines to high-tech beasts.

font-family: 'Exo 2', sans-serif;

A contemporary geometric sans-serif with a futuristic feel. Works well for both display and text.
